Keyboard Shortcuts

  • ALT/Option +I to Convert from Us to India
  • ALT/Option +U to Convert from India to US
  • ALT/Option +1 to Enter US Time
  • ALT/Option +2 to Enter India Time
  • ALT/Option +3 to View Current Time

How It works

  • Standard Time: IST is 11 hours and 30 minutes ahead of Central Standard Time (CST).
  • Daylight Saving Time (DST): During DST, IST is 10 hours and 30 minutes ahead of Central Daylight Time (CDT).

  • Standard Time: IST is 10 hours and 30 minutes ahead of Eastern Standard Time (EST).
  • Daylight Saving Time (DST): During DST, IST is 9 hours and 30 minutes ahead of Eastern Daylight Time (EDT).

  • Standard Time: IST is 12 hours and 30 minutes ahead of Mountain Standard Time (MST).
  • Daylight Saving Time (DST): During DST, IST is 11 hours and 30 minutes ahead of Mountain Daylight Time (MDT).

  • Standard Time: IST is 13 hours and 30 minutes ahead of Pacific Standard Time (PST).
  • Daylight Saving Time (DST): During DST, IST is 12 hours and 30 minutes ahead of Pacific Daylight Time (PDT).
