Privacy Policy

Last Updated January 12 , 2025

US India Time Converter (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy. This document outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and Progressive Web App (PWA).

1. Information We Do Not CollectWe are committed to maintaining a fully anonymous user experience.

2. Third-party Integrations
We use Cloudflare Pages Analytics to monitor website performance and ensure reliability. This analytics service collects only aggregated, non-personal data such as:

•  Total number of visitors
•  Page load times
•  Device types (e.g., mobile or desktop)

This data does not include any PII and is used solely to improve the website’s functionality and performance.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
Our website does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies to store or track user behavior.

4. Offline Capabilities
Our website does not function offline. While a service worker is implemented, it does not provide offline support. An active internet connection is required for the website to function correctly.

5. Children’s Privacy
Our website does not target or restrict use by children of any age. Additionally, the content on the site is family-friendly and suitable for users of all age groups. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13 years of age.

6. Data SecurityThe website does not store any data locally on the user’s device or on cloud servers. Any input provided by users, such as time conversions, is processed in real-time and lost once the page is refreshed. No data is retained or retrievable after the session ends.

7. Changes to This Privacy PolicyWe may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

Features

  • Converts specified times between U.S. time zones and IST
  • Supports all four U.S. time zones—Central , Eastern , Mountain , and Pacific Times
  • Shows Local Time across 4 time zones
  • Offline Support
  • Keyboard shortcuts for quick navigation

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • ALT/Option +I to Convert from Us to India
  • ALT/Option +U to Convert from India to US
  • ALT/Option +1 to Enter US Time
  • ALT/Option +2 to Enter India Time
  • ALT/Option +3 to View Current Time
